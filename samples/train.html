This JSON response is structured for live train tracking systems, combining schedule and GPS feeds:

  • train ‚Äî identifiers, name, operator, overall status, last update timestamp.

  • next_station ‚Äî planned vs. estimated arrival/departure, platform, delay tracking.
  • route_history ‚Äî list of previous stations with timestamps.
