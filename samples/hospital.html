Description:

This HMS sample is organized for easy mapping to relational tables (patient, encounters, orders, medications, labs, appointments, invoices) or to document stores. Fields are intentionally concise but cover the typical clinical + admin workflow.

  • patient — demographics, contacts, emergency contact, allergies, conditions, insurance, consents.
  • encounters — outpatient/inpatient visits with vitals, diagnoses, procedures, orders and notes.
  • medications — active/current meds with strength, route, frequency, start/end.
  • labs — orders and result values with units and ranges.
  • appointments — upcoming bookings with department and status.
  • billing — invoice lines, totals, responsibility, and status.
