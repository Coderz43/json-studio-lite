Description:

This response mirrors what a modern analytics API returns for a dashboard card set. It includes headline metrics (kpis), distribution breakdowns (traffic_sources, devices, geography), goal conversions, top content, tracked events, detected anomalies, and a compact daily timeseries ready for charting libraries.

Design choices

  • Small payload, big coverage: just enough fields to drive bar/line/pie widgets.
  • Consistent keys: short camelCase in series (pv, rev) for faster plotting.
  • Extensible: add channels, segments, or more goals without breaking consumers.

Quick tips

  • Aggregate the timeseries for weekly/monthly views.
  • Flag anomalies by comparing to a moving average or a Prophet forecast.
  • Join top_pages with CMS slugs to create drilldowns.
